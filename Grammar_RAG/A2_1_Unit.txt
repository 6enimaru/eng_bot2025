Unit 1
1.1 Verb to be
Positive
I am
He/She/It is
We are
You are
They are

Negative
I am not
He/She/It isn't
We aren't
You aren't
They aren't

Questions with question words
What is your name? Alicia.
What is her surname? Johnson.
What is his phone number? 07773 321456
Where are you from? London.
Where is she from?
Who is Lara? She's my sister.
Who is she?
How are you? Fine, thanks.
How old are you? I'm 22.

Yes/No questions
Is he/she/it nice? Yes, he is. / No, she isn't.
Are you married? Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
Are they married? Yes, they are. / No, they aren't.

1.2 Possessive adjectives
What's my/your/his/her/its name?
This is our/your/their house.

1.3 Possessive
my wife's name = her name = the name of my wife
Andy's dictionary = his dictionary
my parents' house = their house
